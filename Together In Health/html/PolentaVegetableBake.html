Polenta and Vegetable Bake



Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cups zucchini, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onions, chopped
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 bunch spinch or kale
  • 1 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 polenta role (about 18 ounces), sliced lengthwise into 6 thin slices
  • 1 1/2 cup cheese, shredded
  • 1 lb. ground turkey*
    •

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 450F. Coat a 9-by-13 inch baking dish with oil or cooking spray.

    2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground turkey. Cook until slightly browned.

    3. Add to ground turkey: zucchini, onions, carrots, broccoli, salt, pepper and basil. Cook, stirring occasionally until vegetables are tender, about 4-6 minutes.

    4. Add spinach. Stir until spinach is wilted, about 3 more minutes. Turn off heat.

    5. Add 1 cup marinara sauce, stir until mixed in. Turn off burner.

    6. Place polenta slices in a single layer in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with 3/4 cup cheese.

    7. Add vegetable mixture over polenta, spread to make even. Add remaining 3/4 cup cheese. Top with 1 cup marinara sauce.

    8. Bake until bubbling and cheese has melted, about 12 to 15 minutes.

    9. Let cool, about 5 minutes, serve and enjoy!

    Notes

    1. Pre-portion, store in freezer for healthy frozen meals.

    *Can use 1 lb. ground beef, or one 14 ounce package of tofu (browned and crumbled).
