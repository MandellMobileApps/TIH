Three Bean Chili


Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 medium bunch spinach, rougly chopped
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomatoes, undrained and chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained*
  • 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, rinsed and drained*
  • 1 (15 ounce) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained*
    •
    Instructions

    1. Add 1 Tbsp. olive oil to a large sauce pain, medium heat. Add carrots, celery and onions. Saute until onions are translucent. Add in chopped garlic and spinach. Saute for about 2 minutes, or until spinach is mostly wilted.

    2. Add vegetable broth, canned tomatoes, black beans, cannellini beans and kidney beans to the large sauce pan on medium heat.

    4. Simmer on low heat for 30 about minutes, stirring occasionally.

    5. Serve and enjoy.

    Adding a slice of bread to your meal counts as:

    Notes

    1. Cooked chili will last in the fridge for about 3-5 days.

    2. Pre-portion out chili and put in freezer for healthy frozen meals.

    *Beans count as both a carb and protein in this meal.
