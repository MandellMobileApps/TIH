Zucchini Lasagna


Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis, sliced into thin pieces, length-wise
  • 1 lb. ground beef or turkey
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1 head of broccoli, finely chopped
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 6 white mushrooms, diced
  • 1 large bunch spinach, rougly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (of choice)
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
    •
    Instructions

    1. Place oil in large frying pan, enough to cover the bottom. Add celery, carrots, onions, and broccoli. Cook, stirring occasionally so vegetables don't stick to pan, until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.

    2. In medium size frying pan, add oil, add ground beef or turkey. Cook until meat is cooked through. Add meat to vegetable mixture, mix together.

    3. Cover 9 x 13 inch baking dish with oil or cooking spray. Put thin layer of marinara sauce.

    4. Place zucchini slices until bottom of pan is covered. Spread 1/2 vegetable and meat mixture

    Notes

    1. Casserole freezes well for an easy frozen dinner. Cover uncooked casserole and freeze. Best when eaten within 1-2 months.
