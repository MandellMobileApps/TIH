Panang Chicken Curry



Ingredients

  • 1 6oz can tomato paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 3 tsp. curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes (can add more for more spice)
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 packaged rice cauliflower (1/2 cup per person)
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts*
    •
    Instructions

    1. In a medium size saucepan combine cocnut milk and tomato paste. Turn on low heat and mix until creamy.

    2. Add curry powder and salt, stir until fully mixed in. Leave on low heat.

    3. Cut chicken into 1 inch cubes

    4. Bake chicken for about 35-40 minutes at 400 degrees

    5. While chicken is baking, dice green bell pepper, onion and broccoli. Saute onion in large frying pan for about 5 minutes, then add bell pepper and broccoli. Cook for addition ___ minutes.

    6. Add rice cauliflower to the frying pan, cook for additional ____ minutes

    7. Plate cauliflower and vegeatble mix, add chicken on top and add curry sauce.
    Notes

    Can also use shrimp or tofu as a protein.
