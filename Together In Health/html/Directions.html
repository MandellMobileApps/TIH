Directions
How do I add a meal?
To add a meal and a mood, click on the "+" of the meal you want to add.


Choose the plate that best looks like your meal and the mood that best describes how you are feeling.


The plate and mood will show up in the meal you selected.


How do I add another plate and mood to the same meal?
Click on the "+" for the meal you want to add to. Choose the plate that best looks like your meal and the mood that best describes how you feel. If you went back for seconds, include it as another plate for that meal.


How do I remove a meal?
Swipe to the left to remove a meal.


How do I edit a meal?
Click on the row of the plate you want to edit. Choose the correct plate and/or mood.

How do I add water?
Click on the "+" to add a cup of water. Click on the "-" to remove a cup of water. A cup of water is equal to a standard cup size (about 8 ounces).


Can I add more than 1 cup of water at a time?
Click on the water row.




How do I track my activity?
Click on the "+".


Click on the activity you did or are going to do


Put in the amount of time you did or are going to do the activity for.


How do I edit my time?
Click on the activity you want to edit. Enter in the correct time.

How do I delete an activity?
Swipe to the left to remove an activity.


How do I add a favorite activity?
Click on the Star to add an activity as a favorite activity.


How do I remove a favorite activity?
Click on the Gold Star to remove a favorite activity.


How do I track my goal progress on the calendar?
Click on the day you want to add progress to, a window will pop-up, choose the goal(s) you completed and click "done." To remove a goal select the day you want to remove, the window will pop-up and you can unselect the goal, then click "done."


How does the menu plan work?
For the menu plan you have the option to create your own or choose from pre-made menu plans. Choose "Create Menu Plan" to create your own. You can start your menu plan on the day of your choice. Go through and write in what you will eat for breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks during the week.

Menu Plan 2


Day 1

Breakfast
Tuna melt with 1 slice small or ½ slice large bread, ½ can tuna (try canned in olive oil) mixed with tomatoes and bell peppers and 1 slice of cheese

Snack
1-2 hard-boiled eggs

Lunch
1 large salad with lettuce of choice (mixed greens recommended) with choice of additional vegetables (cucumbers, bell peppers, tomato, radishes), ¼ avocado and 4 oz baked chicken breast, ¼ cup cooked quinoa or peas (optional)

Snack
10 - 15 almonds

Dinner
1-2 Turkey burgers (make enough for leftovers), can use 1 English muffin for the bun or make it protein style with lettuce (romaine or butter lettuce are good options), add salsa instead of ketchup to cut down on added sugars, can also load up with fresh tomatoes, onions and lettuce


Day 2

Breakfast ½ cup homemade granola with ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt

Snack
1 cup sugar snap peas with 1 string cheese

Lunch
Cucumber sandwich (2-3 small cucumbers, cut in half, add 1 Tbsp of cream cheese or hummus and choice of meat)

Snack
½ - 1 cup leftover salad from day 1

Dinner
1 cup Crock pot spicy chicken , can add a side salad or steamed green beans


Day 3

Breakfast
1-2 scrambled eggs with spinach, tomato and basil

Snack
1 large carrot or 6 small carrots with 1 Tbsp 100% peanut butter

Lunch
Leftover turkey burger from day 1

Snack
1-2 hard-boiled eggs

Dinner
Leftover day, eat up whatever is left in the fridge or freezer, if there’s nothing left go with a large mixed salad with tuna, salmon or grilled chicken breast


Day 4

Breakfast
½ cup homemade granola with ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt

Snack
10-15 almonds

Lunch
Leftover crock pot spicy chicken with side of asparagus

Snack
½ bell pepper (color of choice) with 1-2 Tbsp. hummus

Dinner
1 cup Orange chicken with a side of rainbow bell peppers (make it more fun by including red, green orange and yellow bell peppers), to lower the carbs try rice cauliflower, to vary your carbs try with quinoa (follow package instructions to prepare), 1 servings = ½ cup cooked. Make an extra 1 cup dry quinoa for quinoa salad.


Day 5

Breakfast
1 quesadilla with salsa and ¼ avocado

Snack
1 tomato with mozzarella, red wine vinegar, oil and basil

Lunch
Quinoa salad with chicken or tuna

Snack
¼ cup homemade granola

Dinner
Chicken (or tofu) curry with side salad (make enough for breakfast on day 7)


Day 6

Breakfast
1 cup plain Greek yogurt with ½ - 1 cup berries

Snack
10 - 15 almonds

Lunch
1 cup leftover orange chicken with bell peppers

Snack
1 small cucumber (or ⅓ large) and 1-2 Tbsp. hummus

Dinner
Chicken enchiladas with side salad (make half your plate salad)


Day 7

Breakfast
½ - 1 cup leftover chicken curry

Snack
1 small cucumber (or ⅓ large cucumber) with ¼ avocado with vinegar and salt

Lunch
Leftover enchiladas with side salad

Snack
½ cup Quinoa salad

Dinner
1-2 fish tacos (can use lettuce instead of tortillas for low carb option)
