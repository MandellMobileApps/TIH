Protein Packed Macaroni and Cheese



Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 package pasta
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1 package firm tofu



  • Instructions

    1. Pour milk into pot and turn on medium heat. Pour cheese into milk and stir. Heat to a simmer and let cheese melt.

    2. Chop vegetables to chosen size.

    3. Put melted cheese and tofu into blender Hold towel over top of blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.

    4. Pour cooked pasta into ____ pan, pour in vegetables and mix. Pour cheese mixture over pasta and mix.

    5. Bake at ____ for ____ minutes.
