Protein Style Mac and Cheese


Ingredients

  • 1 package pasta (8 oz.)
  • 1 package firm tofu, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 cup milk (or milk substitute)
  • 1 bunch spinch or kale
  • 1 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups cheese, shredded
    •
    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 450F. Coat a 9-by-13 inch baking dish with oil or cooking spray.

    2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add broccoli, stirring occasionally until broccoli is tender, about 4-6 minutes. Add tomatoes and spinach or kale, cook for about 2-3 more minutes. Turn off heat.

    5. Heat milk in large sauce pain, when milk is simmering, add cheese. Turn heat to low, cook until cheese is fully melted.

    6. Add cheese mixture to blender, add tofu, using towel as a lid, blend until creamy.

    7. Cook pasta according to instructions.

    8. Add pasta, vegetables and cheese mixture to baking dish. Mix thoroughly until pasta and vegetables are coated with cheese. Top with a sprinkle of cheese. Place in oven, cook for about 20-30 minutes, until top is slightly browned.
