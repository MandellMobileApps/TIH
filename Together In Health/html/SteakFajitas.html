Steak Fajitas


Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1 medium sweet onion, sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp. fresh coriander, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 8 6-inch flour or corn tortillas
    •
    Instructions

    1. Preheat broiler.

    2. Place steak in shallow baking dish.

    3. Place vegetables in a seperate shallow baking dish with olive oil. Toss vegetables with olive oil so they are fully coated.

    4. For steak marinade: combine garlic, lime juice, oil, vinegar, coriander, cumin and salt in medium bowl. Mix well.

    5. Pour sauce over steak. Toss, cover and refrigerate 1 to 2 hours. (Can prepare and refrigerate the night before or in the morning).

    6. Broil steak 8 to 10 minutes on one side; turn and add rest of marinade. Put shallow pan with vegetables in the oven. Broil for 10 more minutes or until meat is fully cooked and vegetables are slightly browned.

    7. Cut meat at a 45 degree angle into thin slices.

    8. Add meat and vegetables to tortillas. Serve with sour cream, guacamole, and cheese.
