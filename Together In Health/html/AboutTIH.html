About Us
Together In Health was founded on one basic principle: give people the tools and the support they need to be healthy in body, mind and soul. It was developed by a mother daughter team to help people find their individual path to health and happiness.

Why we're different
Just focusing on counting calories, points, carbohydrates, has been shown to not work for long-term success. Together In Health lets you track and see your progress using colors. Balanced eating, and a balanced life, is the key to being healthy and successful. We focus on balancing meals and snacks.



Our Philosphy
Together In Health is not a weight loss program. It doesn't require counting points or counting calories. Why not? Weight loss programs focus on only one very small aspect of health - weight. Together In Health focuses on body, mind and soul. It takes your life, your experiences, your goals all into account. It works with you and your body to help you be healthy from the inside out. It takes into account the latest science from around the world, and what people have known for centuries. And most importantly - your happiness is the real top priority. The best way to start any health program is to love yourself first. Most people find that even after they lose the weight, reach their weight goal, they still don't love themselves fully. That's because we've been taught for a long time that our worth is tied to a number, whether it's a number on a scale or a pants size. We all have the power to take control of our lives and make it what we want, but to be truly successful, we have to love ourselves first.

Meet the Founders
Cami is a clinical dietitian with a Bachelors in Nutrition and a Master of Public Health. She also has worked in public health and corporate wellness. Working on a daily basis with all ages and medical conditions, she works to help people achieve lasting success long after they have reached their goals. She sees daily the struggles people face with both their health and their happiness. She's committed to creating a program that allows people to be truly healthy and happy in a sustainable way.

Nancy is a mother of two with with a very personal experience feeding kids with food allergies and restrictions. She has seen first hand the importance that food has on the body, both good and bad. She sees on a regular basis the effect that food marketing has on food choices and the confusion that surrounds our society's food enviornment. She is committed to creating a program that gives people the information they need to make healthy decisions.
