Snacking Meatballs

 Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef or turkey
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped pine nuts, toasted
  • 1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 small bunch spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
    •
    Instructions

    1. In a large bowl, combine first 6 ingredients. Mix well until all ingredients are blended in. Form into 24 (1 1/2 inch) meatballs.

    2. Heat 1 Tbsp. olive oil in medium skillet over medium-high heat. Place meatballs in sauce pan. Brown meatballs, turning after about 3 minutes; transfer to a plate.

    3. Let cool and enjoy!
    Counts as:


    Notes

    1. Freeze some of the meatballs for easy snack options when you're on-the-go.

    2. Meatballs work great to add as a protein to salad or pasta.
