Garden Vegetable Soup


Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups leaks, chopped
  • 1 cup onions, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp. garlic, finely minced
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups carrots, peeled and chopped (about 2 medium carrots)
  • 2 cups red potatoes, diced
  • 2 cups green beans, broken or cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  • 2 quarts chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 cups tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 ear of corn, kernels removed
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 cup parsley, packed
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
    •
    Instructions

    1. Add olive oil, leeks, garlic and pinch of salt to large stockpot. When onions and leeks begin to soften (about 7 to 8 minutes), add the carrots, potatoes, and green beans. Continue to cook for 4 to 5 more minutes, stiring occasionaly.

    2. Add the stock and bring to a simmer. Once simmering, add tomatoes, corn kernels and pepper.

    3. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook until vegetables are tender, about 25 to 30 minutes.

    4. Remove from heat and add parsley and lemon juice.

    5. Stir, serve and enjoy.

    Notes

    1. This soup will last about 3-5 days in the fridge.

    2. Pre-portion out soup and put in freezer for healthy frozen meals.
