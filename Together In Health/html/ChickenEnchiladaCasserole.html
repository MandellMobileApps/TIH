Chicken Enchilada Casserole


Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. chicken breast tenders, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 small red onion, sliced thin
  • 2 large zucchinis, cut into 1 1/2 x 1/2 inch spears
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 2 cups salsa verde
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 8 6-inch corn tortillas
  • 5 ounces (about 1 cup) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
    •
    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 3-quart shallow baking dish.

    2. In medium skillet, heat 1 Tbsp. olive oil on medium-high. Season chicken with 1/8 tsp. salt and 1/8 tsp. pepper. Add to skillet in single layer and sear for 2 minutes. Turn chicken over, cook for anouther 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer to large bowl.

    3. Add onion to skillet, add more oil as needed. Cook until tender. Add to chicken in bowl.

    4. Add zucchini to skillet, add more oil as needed. Season with 1/8 tsp. salt and 1/8 tsp. pepper. Cook until browned, about 4 minutes. Transfer to bowl with chicken.

    5. Add corn and black beans to skillet, add oil as needed. Stir while cooking until corn is slightly browned, about 3 minutes. Add to chicken in bowl.

    6. Add salsa and sour cream to bowl with chicken. Mix well.

    7. In greased baking dish, arrange 4 tortillas in single layer. Spread 1/2 chicken mixture evenly over tortillas, spread 1/2 of the cheese on top of mixture. Arrange 4 corn tortillas on top of mixture, spread rest of chicken mixture over tortillas. Add remaining cheese.

    8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbling.

    Notes

    1. Casserole freezes well for an easy frozen dinner. Cover uncooked casserole and freeze. Best when eaten within 1-2 months.
