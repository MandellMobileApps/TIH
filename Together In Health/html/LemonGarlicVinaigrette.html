Lemon Garlic Vinaigrette


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 large lemon, juiced
    •
    Instructions

    1. In a sealable container, combine all ingredients. Shake until ingredients are mixed, pour and enjoy!
    Notes

    Make sure to store this salad dressing in the fridge.
